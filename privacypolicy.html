Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or make a purchase from the ABC Center website.

COLLECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION, We collect the following types of personal information when you visit our website: Name Email address Phone number Mailing address Billing information We collect this information when you sign up for our courses or register for our newsletter.

USE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION, We use the personal information that we collect to:

Process your course registration and payments Send you marketing communications about our courses, services, and events Respond to your questions and requests Improve our website and services

SHARING OF PERSONAL INFORMATION, We may share your personal information with the following third parties: Payment processors for payment processing Shipping carriers for course materials delivery Service providers who help us operate our website and improve our services We may also share your personal information to comply with applicable laws and regulations or to respond to a subpoena, search warrant, or other lawful request for information we receive.

YOUR RIGHTS, You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal information that we have collected. You may also withdraw your consent for us to use your personal information at any time by contacting us at abclearningcenter@gmail.com.

SECURITY, We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY, We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

CONTACT US, If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at abclearningcenter@gmail.com.
