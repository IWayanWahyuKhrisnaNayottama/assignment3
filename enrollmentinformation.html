Enrollment Information

Learn how to enroll in ABC Learning Center's courses.

How to Enroll:

  1. Visit the ABC Learning Center website at www.abclearningcenter.com to view our course catalog.
  2. Select the course you are interested in and review the course details, including the description, schedule, and tuition fee.
  3. Fill out the online enrollment form with your personal information, including your name, address, phone number, and email address.
  4. Select the preferred payment option for your tuition fee, which can be paid through credit card or bank transfer.
  5. Submit your enrollment form and wait for a confirmation email from ABC Learning Center within 24 hours.
  6. Once you receive the confirmation email, you will need to log in to your account on the ABC Learning Center website to complete your enrollment.
  7. Upload a copy of your valid ID or passport to confirm your identity.
  8. Review and sign the enrollment agreement, which outlines the terms and conditions of the course enrollment.
  9. Complete the course orientation and assessment, which will be provided through the ABC Learning Center online platform.
  10. Start your course and participate in the learning activities and assessments as directed by your course instructor.

If you need help with the enrollment process, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.
